Brazil Travel Checklist

A quick list for European travelers

Before You Leave

  • Check passport validity (at least 6 months beyond your stay).
  • Confirm visa requirements for your nationality.
  • Purchase travel insurance that covers health and emergencies.
  • Consult a doctor about recommended vaccinations (e.g., yellow fever).

Packing Essentials

  • Lightweight clothing and swimwear for warm weather.
  • Sun protection: sunscreen, hat and sunglasses.
  • Power adapter (Brazil uses types N and C, 127/220V).
  • A small amount of local currency for arrival expenses.

On Arrival

  • Keep copies of your passport and travel documents.
  • Use official taxis or rideshare apps from the airport.
  • Stay hydrated and apply insect repellent when needed.
