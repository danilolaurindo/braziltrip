Brazil 22-Day Adventure

An extended journey exploring Brazil's highlights

Day 1: Dec 12 – Depart from Helsinki

  • Check in early at HEL Airport and enjoy a hearty breakfast.
  • Browse duty-free shops for last-minute travel goodies.
  • Settle in for the overnight flight to São Paulo.

Day 2: Dec 13 – Exploring São Paulo

  • Walk the vibrant Avenida Paulista and visit cultural centers.
  • Relax in Ibirapuera Park’s green spaces and museums.
  • Sample exotic fruits at the Municipal Market for lunch.

Day 3: Dec 14 – More of São Paulo

  • Visit the São Paulo Museum of Art (MASP) on Paulista.
  • Stroll around the historic Sé Cathedral and downtown squares.
  • Experience São Paulo’s nightlife in Vila Madalena.

Day 4: Dec 15 – Last Day in São Paulo

  • Explore the Japanese district of Liberdade for lunch and shopping.
  • Admire street art in Beco do Batman alley.
  • Sip rich Brazilian coffee at a specialty café.

Day 5: Dec 16 – Fly to Manaus

  • Catch a morning flight from São Paulo to Manaus.
  • Visit the ornate Amazon Theatre downtown.
  • Dine on fresh tambaqui fish at a local market.

Day 6: Dec 17 – Into the Amazon

  • Head out on a guided jungle hike to learn about native flora.
  • Take a boat tour to spot wildlife along the Amazon River.
  • Try piranha fishing or caiman spotting after dark.

Day 7: Dec 18 – Amazon Exploration

  • Visit an indigenous community to learn about local traditions.
  • Paddle a canoe through flooded forests and narrow creeks.
  • Witness the Meeting of the Waters near Manaus.

Day 8: Dec 19 – Amazon Adventures

  • Go birdwatching at sunrise with a local guide.
  • Learn jungle survival skills using medicinal plants.
  • Take a refreshing swim beneath a jungle waterfall.

Day 9: Dec 20 – Last Day in the Jungle

  • Walk a canopy trail or try a zip line adventure.
  • Search for pink river dolphins on a river excursion.
  • Relax at your jungle lodge and share stories around a fire.

Day 10: Dec 21 – Travel to Fortaleza

  • Fly from Manaus to sunny Fortaleza on the coast.
  • Take an evening stroll along lively Beira-Mar Avenue.
  • Feast on fresh seafood at the beachfront fish market.

Day 11: Dec 22 – Beach Life Begins

  • Relax on Fortaleza’s famous Praia do Futuro.
  • Try kitesurfing or paddleboarding in the warm waters.
  • Dance to live forró music at night.

Day 12: Dec 23 – More Beach Time

  • Take a day trip to Cumbuco for sand dunes and buggy rides.
  • Sip coconut water under a beach umbrella.
  • Visit the Dragão do Mar cultural center in the evening.

Day 13: Dec 24 – Coastal Explorations

  • Head out to Canoa Quebrada for dramatic red cliffs.
  • Try horseback riding along the shoreline.
  • Watch the sunset from the dunes with a cold drink.

Day 14: Dec 25 – Christmas on the Beach

  • Spend the morning relaxing at Lagoinha Beach.
  • Take a short boat ride to nearby lagoons.
  • Enjoy a festive dinner of seafood and local treats.

Day 15: Dec 26 – Sun and Surf

  • Snorkel around the reefs of nearby beaches.
  • Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch right on the sand.
  • Watch a capoeira performance at sunset.

Day 16: Dec 27 – Final Day on the Coast

  • Start the morning with relaxing yoga on the beach.
  • Shop for local crafts and souvenirs at a beach market.
  • Sail at sunset for stunning coastal views.

Day 17: Dec 28 – Return to São Paulo

  • Fly back to São Paulo from Fortaleza.
  • Explore colorful street art in Vila Madalena.
  • Enjoy dinner at a classic Brazilian churrascaria.

Day 18: Dec 29 – Art & Nature in SP

  • Visit the Pinacoteca de São Paulo art museum.
  • Take a walk in Horto Florestal park on the city’s edge.
  • Taste artisanal coffee at the famous Coffee Lab.

Day 19: Dec 30 – Markets and Street Art

  • Grab a famous mortadella sandwich at the Municipal Market.
  • Tour vibrant street murals around Beco do Batman.
  • Take in city views from the top of Edifício Copan.

Day 20: Dec 31 – New Year’s Eve in SP

  • Take a day trip to the artsy town of Embu das Artes.
  • Learn about Brazil’s passion at the Football Museum.
  • Celebrate New Year’s Eve with fireworks on Avenida Paulista.

Day 21: Jan 1 – Discover More of SP

  • Stroll through Parque da Independência and museum.
  • Shop on stylish Oscar Freire Street for souvenirs.
  • Enjoy a farewell dinner in a traditional restaurant.

Day 22: Jan 2 – Depart from GRU

  • Pack your bags and check out of the hotel.
  • Head to Guarulhos International Airport for the flight home.
  • Look back on the amazing adventures of Brazil.

Enjoy your extended stay in Brazil!
